Macabre Morii

Story »

   You play as Laykha Balindong. Struggling for inspiration, you end up in a museum by an artist only
known as "Celda". The museum has only a few attendees, though unsual as the artist was relatively
popular. As you explore their artworks you see 5 stone statues as their star exhibit. "Red, Orange,
Yellow, Green, Blue." You feel uncomfortable yet feel inspired to draw them and their unique styles.
You turn your back to the exhibit. The lights turn off. You turn back confused. The statues aren't
there anymore. You look around the museum and find 4 people and 1 dead body. Find who did it and
escape. Your decisions will affect the story.
